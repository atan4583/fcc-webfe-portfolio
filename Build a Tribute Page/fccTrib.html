a wondrous friend who is always giving to others unconditionally

Here are 10 fun facts about Christopher R :

  • he turns 4 on Apr 29
  • he has an omnivorous mind but comes to his food, he is 101% herbivorous
  • he is extraordinary kind, generous and giving
  • he is witty, original and hilariously funny
  • he has a genuine appreciation of other ethnic histories and cultures, and takes a keen interest in that of India
  • he is not a saint though; just like everyone else, he can have his bad moments, run out of patience, mutter some choice words and tell you to go fly a kite
  • he loves Beach Slang punk rock but also likes Bollywood music
  • he is a keen photographer, avid art museums lover, moviegoers and genius coder
  • he is multilingual with an astonishing vocabulary in his non native tongue English
  • he lives to eat, travel, helping people and having fun

In the big wide world, Christopher R is simply an ordinary bloke, but to those in his life, the world is a better place because of his existence. This page was created solely to mark the magical day he was dropped off the sky by the stork some 4 years ago.

  Happy Birthday, Christopher R !!!
